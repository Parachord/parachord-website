Plugging The Big Idea

Stop chasing music across platforms.


Given metadata about a song, album, or playlist, Parachord finds the best available source for you and just plays it.


A new kind of music player that invites all your streams, downloads, playlists, recommendations and friends to the same party. It's about time they all mingle.

Why Parachord

Experiences you can't get anywhere else

Find the rare stuff

That live recording only on YouTube. The Bandcamp-exclusive EP. The vinyl rip in your collection. Parachord finds them all and lets you listen to, collect, and playlist them together.

Prioritize your way

Prioritize your service plug-ins however you want. Whether that is Bandcamp as the default service, backfilled by a streaming service or one streaming service over the other. It's up to you.

Break through the paralysis of choice

See what friends are playing and just hitch a ride while they drive the aux.

Your recommendations engines

Plug in Last.fm, ListenBrainz, or AI engines. It's up to you which ones - or all of them. Get suggestions from sources you trust, not black-box algorithms.

Translatation layer

Someone sent you a Spotify link but you use YouTube? Paste it in. Parachord plays it from your sources - not theirs.
