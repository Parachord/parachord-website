Features

Many Music Sources

Connect your favorite streaming services and local libraries, then prioritize them however you like. Parachord seamlessly switches between sources so your music never stops — no manual juggling required.

Friends & Listen Along

Real human curation. See what friends are playing (in whatever apps they use!), browse their libraries, and listen along in real-time. Music is better together.

Sync Your Library & Favorites

Sync favorites and libraries from your existing services. Your carefully curated collection, unified in one place.

Shuffleupagus AI

AI-powered music recommendations from the engines you choose - ChatGPT, Claude, Gemini, or all of them at once. Your taste, amplified.

Music Service Interoperability

Someone shared a Spotify link but you use Apple Music? Use the browser extension to easily import and play it from your sources. Parachord translates between services so you can play anything from anywhere.

Smartlinks & Embeds

Embeddable buttons and smartlinks for artists, labels, and blogs. Let fans play music from their preferred service, right from your site.

New Releases

Stay on top of what's dropping. Fresh releases from your favorite artists and curated charts, aggregated across all your connected services.

Recommendations

Discover new music through personalized recommendations — by artist or by song. Powered by your listening history and refined by the engines you trust.

Smart Queue Management & Spin-Offs

Full control over what plays next. Drag, reorder, and spin off new playlists from your queue on the fly. Your listening session becomes a living playlist.

Critical Darlings

The most acclaimed albums from across the web - collected and presented to you with overviews of what the critics are saying.

Bandcamp Buy Buttons

Support the artists you love. Bandcamp purchase links appear in the Now Playing view for the songs Parachord can find there - regardless of where you are streaming it from, so you can buy music directly without leaving Parachord.

Search & Browse

Explore artist pages, biographies, discographies, EPs, singles, and related artists. Deep-dive into any artist's catalog with rich metadata from multiple sources.

Why Parachord

Experiences you can't get anywhere else

Find the rare stuff

That live recording only on YouTube. The Bandcamp-exclusive EP. The vinyl rip in your collection. Parachord finds them all and lets you listen to, collect, and playlist them together.

Prioritize your way

Prioritize your service plug-ins however you want. Whether that is Bandcamp as the default service, backfilled by a streaming service or one streaming service over the other. It's up to you.

Break through the paralysis of choice

See what friends are playing and just hitch a ride while they drive the aux.

Your recommendation engines

Plug in Last.fm, ListenBrainz, or AI engines. It's up to you which ones - or all of them. Get suggestions from sources you trust, not black-box algorithms.

Translation layer

Someone sent you a Spotify link but you use YouTube? Paste it in. Parachord plays it from your sources - not theirs.

Own your playlists

Standard XSPF format. Import, convert, export, share, and keep your curation forever—even if platforms disappear.
